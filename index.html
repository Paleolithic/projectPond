Hey there, welcome to ProjectPond. We're in the process of making the application, but we'll keep in touch with updates.

Finding talented people can be hard, especially on your own. ProjectPond brings skilled people like you together to collaborate on projects of all shapes and sizes. Tell us what you’re good at and we’ll help you find something to do. Or share your project with the world and build the team that you need make your idea into a reality. User Reviews and Peer Endorsements let you know you’re getting the best possible teammates and allow you to hold each other accountable. Best of all you don’t have to pay for anything when you sign up with a student email!
